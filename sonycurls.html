sonycurls
the brand
Founded by SÃ³nia Branco, sonycurls is a haircare page for curly hair. Its digital content includes video tutorials, product reviews, hairstyle and DIY haircare ideas, as well as lifestyle and inspiration photography.
communication
Sonycurls intends to be a very casual and personal brand, that seeks to relate and share with its audience, fitting in perfectly in the spirit of the curly hair community, famous for mutual support and sharing of advice and personal experiences.
The cursive logo is allusive to the theme of curly hair. The short version should be used in situations where the other version is too big or won't fit correctly.
colors
I chose light and vibrant colors, for a fresh, clean and summery look, used against white.
art direction
The used photography is light and saturated, using a lot of natural elements and inspiring landscapes, for a visually appealing experience.

mobile experience

hero and menu
The menu is divided in different sections, such as products, hairstyles, about, contacts, inspiration, for easy navigation. It also includes the brand's social media at the bottom.
posts
Featured posts are right below the hero, with their main image and a small preview paragraph - you can easily check out the most interesting ones right away. Once you hit the "read" button, it will redirect you to the full post page.

desktop experience

navigation
There are two navigation bars - one for the about page and contacts, and the shop at the top right corner; and the other for the hair specific topics - this was thought for easier and more organized navigation.
new here, where do i start?
Many regular posts may mention some concepts and vocabulary that a complete "beginner" to curly hair care would not understand. For a beginner friendly experience, this article is displayed and linked in the homepage, and it explains the most basic notions that one should be aware of when reading about this subject. It contains illustrations and helpful videos that explain the techniques and some of the science behind curly hair and how to care for it.
curly guide
The curly guide is a section with posts dedicated to the different hair washing and brushing techniques, protective treatments, hair masks, and everything else that helps keeping curly hair beautiful and healthy.
products and reviews
Product posts have a rating, so you can quickly know how she feels about this product. Below the post are some suggestions of other products reviewed previously.
