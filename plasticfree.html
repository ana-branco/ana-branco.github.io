plastic free design club
the brand
Plastic Free Design Club was a group project that I was part of, created towards sustainability and awareness of plastic and it's impacts on the environment, with designers and artists as its target audience. With an identity established, me and a fellow web designer collaborated to design a web site for this movement.

web page

home and design
To showcase the colorful and vibrant stamps that were created for this brand, we decided to keep a simple black background, and very clean and neat interface throughout the whole website. Page titles and different sections are signaled with this pop art vibrant stamps, but the rest remains simple and clean.
database and memberpage
Here are all the artists and designers that adhered to this movement, each square with one of their projects. Upon clicking it, it will redirect to that designer's personal page or portfolio. You can also use the filters above to search some specific design areas.
filtering and searching
By selecting a specific type of design from the filters above - like product design for example - the squares relative to a designer from that category will light up, making for an easy serach.
joining the movement
From the "join" button in the hero, and throughout other instances on the website, an artist or designer that would like to join the movement would be lead to a form page. After filling it and completing the process, a thank you page will show up.
thank you page
This page shows up after completing your registration to the site database, fist like the image above, grayer, with more plastic in the ocean and the sea level higher, before changing to a more colorful version, with less plastic, as to transmit the impact that each person can have on the environment.
shop
Shows the club's merchandise, so that you can wear it with pride! Stickers, chains, and t-shirts, all of them in biodegradable and environmentally friendly materials.
